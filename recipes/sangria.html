Sangria

Description

A wine-based drink enjoyed in many parts of Spain, including Huelva.

Very refreshing on a summer's day.

Ingredients

  • Bit of red wine
  • Bit of fizzy pop
  • Dash of fruit

Instructions

  1. Combine bit of red wine and bit of fizzy pop in a large pitcher
  2. Add a dash of fruit to pitcher
  3. Stir contents of pitcher with a spoon or ladle to combine
  4. Pour into glasses and enjoy!
