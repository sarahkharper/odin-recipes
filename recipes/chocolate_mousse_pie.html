Falling in Love Chocolate Mousse Pie

Description

The perfect pie to wish a friend (or yourself!) good luck on romantic endeavours.

Ingredients

  • (1) 9-inch baked pastry shell
  • (1) 4 oz can condensed milk
  • 2/3 cup water
  • 1 oz unsweetened chocolate
  • 2 cups whipping cream, whipped

Directions

  1. In a large saucepan, combine condensed milk, water, and pudding mix; mix well
  2. Add chocolate
  3. Cook over medium heat, stirring rapidly until chocolate melts and mixture thickens
  4. Remove from heat and beat until smooth
  5. Let cool thoroughly
  6. Once cool, fold whipped cream into chocolate mixture
  7. Pour mixture into prepared pastry shell and chill for 4 hours until set
